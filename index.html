TRENT MULDER

Meet Trent Mulder

Prior to becoming a real estate agent, Trent was born and raised in Ontario, CA. He has spent most of his life living in Southern California and graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Physics at Point Loma Nazarene University, located in San Diego, CA. Before becoming a real estate agent, he has worked as a Manufacturing Engineer at Vertechs Enterprises, General Atomics, and Northrop Grumman.

His interpersonal and analytical skills that were acquired from his time as a Principal Manufacturing Engineer, has led to his interests in immersing himself into the real estate world. Trent's work ethic and deep commitment towards helping others has proven to give him leverage over others in the industry.

When he is not working on real estate, Trent can be found running, weightlifting, surfing or fixing cars. He also enjoys going on cruises and spending time at the beach. With both his analytical skills as an engineer and his love for prime real estate, he is committed to giving his clients the best options possible.
